## 1. Welcome

Flavor Entertainers is a booking platform for non-contact entertainment in Western Australia. Your safety — physical, emotional, and financial — is the platform's primary responsibility.

This briefing is mandatory before your profile goes live. Please read it in full. You'll be asked to acknowledge that you've understood and agreed to the safety practices outlined here. The acknowledgement is logged for compliance.

## 2. Bookings you accept

- You can decline any booking for any reason. You don't have to give a reason — just decline in the dashboard. We will not penalise you.
- Once you accept a booking and the deposit is paid, the client receives the venue address and your stage name. Your real name and direct contact details are never released to clients.
- If something feels off about a booking before it starts (unclear venue, last-minute changes, third-party booker), you can cancel from your dashboard without penalty.

## 3. The no-touch policy

Unless explicitly negotiated and agreed in writing through the platform's chat, **no physical contact is permitted**. The default contract is non-contact entertainment only.

If a client breaches no-touch:

1. **End the booking immediately.** Your safety comes first. You are not obligated to complete the engagement.
2. Use the **Flag customer** button in the booking view (red flag icon).
3. Choose "Breached no-touch policy" as the reason.
4. The customer's contact details are added (hashed) to the Do-Not-Serve register within seconds. They will not be able to book again.
5. Notify admin via the dashboard "Help" button — admin can dispatch additional support if needed.

You will still be paid the full deposit for any booking ended early due to a breach.

## 4. Intoxicated or aggressive clients

You are entitled to refuse service to any client who is:

- Visibly intoxicated beyond reasonable enjoyment (slurring, falling, unable to consent to the booking parameters)
- Aggressive, threatening, or disrespectful in language or body language
- Bringing additional unannounced guests not in the original booking
- Photographing or filming you without prior written consent

Use the **Flag customer** button with "Intoxicated / aggressive" as the reason. End the booking. Document what happened in the notes field.

## 5. Payment

- The deposit (25% of total) is paid before you arrive. You see this confirmed in your dashboard before leaving home.
- The remaining balance is collected by you in cash on arrival, before performance begins. **Do not start the performance without the balance in hand.**
- If a client refuses to pay the balance, end the engagement, flag the customer with "Refused payment", and contact admin. You'll keep the deposit.

## 6. Travel

- Bookings outside the Perth metro area attract a travel fee ($1/km over 50km from CBD), automatically calculated and added to the deposit. You don't need to chase this.
- Always share your live location with a trusted contact during a booking. Most performers use WhatsApp or iMessage location sharing for the duration of the engagement.
- The platform does not currently provide a panic button — if you are in immediate danger, call 000.

## 7. Health & wellbeing

- You are an independent contractor. The platform doesn't dictate your hours, but we strongly recommend not accepting back-to-back bookings without breaks.
- If you're feeling unwell, mark yourself unavailable in the dashboard. Existing bookings can be reassigned by admin.
- If a booking leaves you shaken or distressed, contact admin support — we can refer you to industry-friendly counselling services.

## 8. Privacy & data

The platform handles your data as follows:

- **Government ID**: viewed once by admin during onboarding; deleted from storage within 30 seconds of the review decision. We retain only a record that "this performer was reviewed and approved" — not the ID itself.
- **Liveness embedding**: a 128-dimensional numerical vector derived from a brief blink-and-look check. No image is uploaded or stored.
- **Bank details**: tokenised by our payments provider (Monoova). We store only an opaque token reference — never your raw BSB or account number.
- **Phone and email**: stored hashed (HMAC-SHA256) wherever possible; raw values only where required for messaging.
